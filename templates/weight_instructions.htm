HIR - Health Insurance Recommender

Please add a weight to each service you select with more weight to higher priority service, such that sum of all the weights add up exactly equal to 100

For example- You select Abortion, dental care and eye care. If weight of dental care = 50. weight of eye care = 30. weight of abortion = 20, then your priority for dental care would be highest and that for abortion would be lowest among the selected services.
